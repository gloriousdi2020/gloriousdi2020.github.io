The Creative Hub

The Creative Hub welcomes young kids like you who is creative and loves art. It is a place for you to find inspiration and be inspired by the other artists. Art is everywhere to tell a story, adds color and lives to the place. But it is often taken for granted. The Creative Hub encourage you to take a step back and take in your surroundings across Brisbane. Art galleries and street art is a great starting point to understand the culture of a place.
