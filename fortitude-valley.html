Kaff-eine Street Art | Brisbane Street Art Festival 2021 | Fortitude Valley

Kaff-eine participated in the Brisbane Street Art Festival 2021 and created a wall art at Fortitude Valley. Kaff-eine is a female Australian artist who paints public artwork globally. She focuses on trendy social topics and inspire viewers to be more involved in social and political issues. Wall art can be found here at The Wickham, 308 Wickham St, Fortitude Valley.
