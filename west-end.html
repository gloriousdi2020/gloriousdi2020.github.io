Pillars Project | G20 Celebrations 2014 | West End

The Pillars Project was started in 2014 by eight local artists. It is known to be a highlight of street art in West End. Artists include Guido Van Helton, Gus Eagleton, John Beer (aka Gimiks Born), Simon Degroot, Fintan Magee and Mik Shida. Street art can be found here around the corner at Montague Rd & Merivale St.
